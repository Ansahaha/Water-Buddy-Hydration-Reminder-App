
#  Water Buddy

Water Buddy is a mobile hydration tracking application designed to encourage healthy water-drinking habits through a simple, engaging, and interactive user experience.

The app features **Poco**, a friendly cat companion who motivates users to stay hydrated throughout the day. Users can log their water intake and watch their progress visually fill a water container, making hydration tracking more enjoyable and rewarding.

---

##  About the Project

Many people struggle to meet their daily hydration goals because they simply forget to drink water during busy schedules.

Water Buddy was created as a personal UI/UX design project to explore how gamification, visual feedback, and character-driven interactions can improve user engagement in health-related applications.

The concept focuses on turning a routine habit into a fun experience through the use of an animated mascot and progress-based rewards.

---

##  Features

* Water intake logging
* Hydration progress tracking
* Percentage-based completion indicator
* Interactive mascot character (Poco)
* Clean and beginner-friendly interface
* Visual feedback through water-level animations
* Multiple hydration states and character reactions
